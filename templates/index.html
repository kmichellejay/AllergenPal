AllergenPal

Navigate dietary restrictions effortlessly with AllergenPal, your personalized tool for filtering food items based on your specific allergies or dietary needs. Simply enter the URL or PDF link of a restaurant's menu along with your dietary restrictions or allergens. AllergenPal will comb through the menu and notify you of any items that might contain potential allergen triggers. Dining out with peace of mind is just a few clicks away with AllergenPal. Start today, and let AllergenPal make your dining experiences safer and smoother.

How To Use:

  1. Enter your Menu URL or PDF link: Input the web address of a restaurant's menu or a PDF link directly into the input field.
  2. List Allergens or Dietary Restrictions: Fill in your dietary restrictions or allergens in the next field. Please separate each allergen with a comma.
  3. Submit: Once you've filled in both fields, click the 'Check Allergens' button. AllergenPal will process the menu and list any items that might contain your specified allergens.

Our AI will analyze the menu and check for potential allergens.
